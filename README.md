**PPMK가자** is a centralized, modern digital hub designed specifically for the Malaysian student community in South Korea. By blending Malaysian community needs with Korean student digital culture, the platform streamlines communication, enhances transparency in elections, and fosters a stronger sense of belonging.

## 🚀 Inspiration
Malaysian students in Korea often struggle with fragmented communication. Critical event updates frequently get lost in spam folders or fast-moving KakaoTalk chats. Additionally, traditional processes for elections and volunteering applications often lack transparency. We envisioned a single, trusted "central hub" to connect students and modernize student association management.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Verified Community:** Student-only access via email and student ID verification.
* **Event Management:** A clean interface for bulletins and RSVP systems, featuring **QR-based attendance tracking**.
* **Community Forums:** Dedicated spaces for university-specific news, housing, food, pets, and marketplaces.
* **대나무숲 (Bamboo Forest):** An anonymous posting board inspired by Korean university culture.
* **AI-Enhanced Elections:** * **Live Voting Tracker:** Real-time accountability for AGM elections.
    * **AI Role Recommender:** Suggests suitable committee roles based on student profiles.
    * **Candidate Comparisons:** Ability to rate and compare candidates for fairer evaluations.
* **Gamified Profiles:** Achievement badges, contribution stats, and role tracking to encourage community engagement.

## 🛠️ Built With
* **Languages:** TypeScript, JavaScript
* **Frontend/Structure:** HTML5, CSS3
* **Data/Configuration:** JSON, XML

## 🎨 Design & UX
* **Modern Aesthetic:** A dark-themed interface with clean accents and "Gen Z-friendly" visuals.
* **Smooth Navigation:** Left-side navigation bar with emoji-based iconography.
* **Cultural Fusion:** Blends Malaysian community requirements with Korean digital trends (e.g., pixel-style accents and anonymity features).

## 💪 Challenges & Accomplishments
* **Challenges:** Balancing professional utility with engaging visuals; implementing a secure yet simple voting mechanism; ensuring smooth navigation across diverse features.
* **Accomplishments:** Created a first-of-its-kind central hub for PPMK; introduced AI-enhanced election transparency; successfully implemented a QR-based attendance system.

## 📈 What We Learned
* The value of designing with **cultural context** (Korean + Malaysian elements).
* The importance of verification and trust in building digital student communities.
* How **gamification** (badges/roles) drives participation and retention.

## 🔮 What's Next
* **Official Launch:** Onboarding members across multiple universities in South Korea.
* **Management Tools:** Adding ticketing, automated reminders, and push notifications.
* **Mobile App:** Developing a native mobile version for better accessibility.
* **DAO Governance:** Exploring decentralized governance models for student association decision-making.
